Session Planner — How to Use

Session Builder

Create a practice session by adding timed blocks. Each block has a label (e.g. "Scales"), a duration, and a colour. Reorder blocks with the arrow buttons, or delete with ✕.

Templates

Use the preset template pills for quick session plans:

  • Quick 30 — 5 min Warmup, 15 min Repertoire, 10 min Technique
  • Full Hour — 10 min Warmup, 10 min Scales, 20 min Repertoire, 10 min Sight Reading, 10 min Ear Training
  • Focused 20 — 5 min Warmup, 15 min Deep Practice

Timer Controls

Press ▶ to start the countdown. The timer works through each block in order. Use |◀ to restart the current block, or ▶| to skip ahead. A chime plays between blocks.

Session Log

Completed sessions are automatically logged. The This Week summary shows your total minutes, number of sessions, and current streak of consecutive practice days.

Tap the 📊 icon in the top bar to view a 14-day bar chart of your practice history.

Persistence

Your block list, theme choice, and practice log are saved to localStorage. Everything works offline.

💡 The timer keeps running even if you switch themes. Your session is safe!
