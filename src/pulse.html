Pulse
Visual Metronome — How to Use

Transport & BPM

Press the large play button or hit Space to start/stop. Use ±1/±10 nudge buttons or arrow keys to adjust BPM. Tap the Tap button (or press T) rhythmically to detect tempo from performance.

Presets

Save up to 5 preset slots to quickly recall your favourite configurations. Each preset stores BPM, time signature, subdivision, accents, swing, sound settings, and click rate.

Save: click the 💾 Save button — all slots start pulsing. Click a slot to save the current config. Press Escape or click ✕ Cancel to exit save mode.

Load: click any filled slot to instantly load that preset (stops playback first).

Rename: right-click (or long-press on mobile) a filled slot to give it a custom name (max 12 characters).

Beat Visualizer

The beat grid shows all beats in the bar. Click any beat dot to cycle through three accent levels — normal, medium accent, and strong accent. Accent level affects both click volume and visual brightness. Subdivision dots can also be clicked to accent them independently, stepping through quiet, medium, and strong levels; their exact loudness follows your global Accent Boost setting.

Pendulum Arm

A canvas-drawn mechanical pendulum arm swings in sync with the beat. Toggle visibility with the small Arm chip at the bottom of the main panel.

Time Signature & Feel

Choose a preset or configure manually. The ½× / 1× / 2× chip in the main panel halves or doubles the effective click speed without changing the BPM readout. Swing delays every even subdivision for a shuffle or jazz feel.

Sound Shaping

Brightness: from a muted woody thud to a crisp sharp click. Envelope: short = punchy & percussive, long = fuller & rounder. Accent Boost: how much louder accented beats hit.

Speed Trainer

Enable the Speed Trainer card to practice gradual tempo increases. Set a start and end BPM, choose Bars or Minutes mode, and press ▶ Start. The metronome ramps linearly from start to end BPM. A progress bar shows how far through the ramp you are. When complete, the metronome stays at the end BPM.

Count-In

Toggle Count-in to get one bar of clicks before normal operation begins. During the count-in bar the beat grid stays still so you can prepare, and Count-in… appears in the tempo display.

Mute-Bar Training

Toggle Mute bars to alternate between audible and silent bars. Use the stepper to set how many bars play before the same number are muted. During silent bars the beat dots still animate so you can check your internal timing — the grid dims slightly as a visual cue.

💡 Keyboard: Space = play/stop · Arrow keys = ±1/5 BPM · T = tap tempo
